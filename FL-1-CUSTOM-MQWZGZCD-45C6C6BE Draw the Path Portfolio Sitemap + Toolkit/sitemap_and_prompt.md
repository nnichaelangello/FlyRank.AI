## 2. Claude Project Setup
**Project Name:** AI Portfolio Build
**Custom Instructions:**
```text
Act as my portfolio tutor and editor. My proof statement is: "I can architect, train, and deploy production-ready Artificial Intelligence and Federated Learning systems. I am building this portfolio to prove this to Tech Leads and Technical Recruiters at top-tier AI companies and innovative startups so that they will email me directly to schedule a technical interview or project discussion." Keep your advice sharp, direct, and aligned with proving this specific claim to this specific audience.
```

## 3. The Pressure-Test Prompt
*Run this prompt in your new Claude Project and save the output screenshot.*

**Prompt:**
> Here is my planned portfolio sitemap:
> 1. Home (Hero claim + quick bio)
> 2. Work (Case studies on CV, Federated Learning, and ML)
> 3. About (Publications & Academic background)
> 4. Contact (Email CTA)
> 
> Pressure-test this sitemap against my proof statement and my one action (getting tech leads to email me for an interview). Is there any fluff here? Is there friction preventing them from taking action? Tell me one thing I should change to make it tighter.
