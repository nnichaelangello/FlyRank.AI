## Case Study 1: AI-Based CCTV Workforce Tracking
**The Problem:** PT Ajinomoto Indonesia needed a reliable, non-intrusive way to monitor the exact positions and activities of factory workers across specific operational zones, 24/7, without relying on manual check-ins.
**What I Did & Decided:** I engineered a custom computer vision pipeline using YOLOv8 for person detection and EasyOCR to dynamically read jersey numbers. I decided to build a high-performance multi-threaded backend in Python using FastAPI and WebSockets to process continuous video streams without memory bottlenecks.
**The Outcome:** The system successfully deployed in production, providing stable, 24/7 automated zone tracking and attendance logging for the factory workforce.

## Case Study 2: AKSARA Learning (AI for Dyslexia)
**The Problem:** Students with dyslexia struggle in traditional classroom environments because standard text-based materials lack the multisensory engagement they need to process information effectively.
**What I Did & Decided:** I developed a web-based inclusive learning platform that adapts to the student. I integrated Text-to-Speech (TTS), Speech-to-Text (STT), and OCR models directly into the workflow to turn static text into interactive, multisensory lessons.
**The Outcome:** The platform was successfully field-tested and implemented in special needs schools (SLB Bina Mandiri). It also won 2nd Place at the National Student Digital Innovation Competition (LIDM 2025).

## Case Study 3: PRISMA (Federated Learning Major Recommendation)
**The Problem:** Training a machine learning model to recommend university majors requires vast amounts of student academic data, which creates significant privacy and data-sharing risks.
**What I Did & Decided:** I architected a privacy-preserving recommendation engine using Federated Learning. I used Node.js and Next.js for the platform and implemented a Federated Averaging algorithm with Neural Networks. This allowed the model to train decentrally on local student data without ever transferring raw personal data to the central server.
**The Outcome:** Successfully demonstrated a secure, scalable way to provide data-driven major recommendations while mathematically guaranteeing user privacy.

## Case Study 4: FlyRank.AI (Search Intelligence)
**The Problem:** As a machine learning intern, I needed to evaluate how varying dataset features impact predictive accuracy for search intelligence rankings.
**What I Did & Decided:** I framed the research question, ran extensive baseline experiments using decision tree classifiers, and established a reproducible ML pipeline to evaluate ranking opportunities.
**The Outcome:** Delivered structured ML task framing and validated baseline models that lay the groundwork for advanced ranking algorithms at FlyRank.
