# FL-5: Identity Kit

## 1. Typography (Fonts)
- **Heading Font:** *Inter* (Clean, modern sans-serif for high readability in titles).
- **Body Font:** *Roboto Mono* (A monospace font to give a technical, "code-editor" feel appropriate for an AI/ML developer).

## 2. Color Palette (Dark Mode Aesthetic)
- **Background:** Deep Navy (`#0A192F`) - Calm, authoritative, and easy on the eyes.
- **Main Text:** Slate White (`#CCD6F6`) - High contrast but softer than pure white.
- **Accent Color:** Electric Cyan (`#64FFDA`) - A striking, futuristic highlight color used sparingly for buttons and links.

## 3. Logo / Favicon
A clean, text-based monogram styled like code:
**`< MA />`** 
*(Rendered in Roboto Mono, colored in Electric Cyan)*

## 4. Two-Line Style Note (For Claude Project)
> **Style Note:** "Use a dark, highly technical, code-editor aesthetic (Deep Navy background, Electric Cyan accents, Roboto Mono body text). The mood is focused, academic, and purely functional, ensuring that the colorful screenshots of my computer vision projects are the loudest things on the page."
